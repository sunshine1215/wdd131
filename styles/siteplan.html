🐾 Happy Pets – Site Plan

WDD 131 – Website Planning Document

1. Site Name

Happy Pets

I chose this name because it clearly communicates the goal of the website — helping pet owners keep their animals healthy and happy. The name is simple, friendly, and easy to remember for all ages.

Optional domain: happypets-guide.com

2. Site Purpose

Happy Pets is an informational website designed to help pet owners care for their dogs and cats. The site provides basic pet care tips covering feeding, grooming, exercise, health, and safety. It also includes an interactive pet care quiz to help users test their knowledge and learn more about responsible pet ownership.

3. Scenarios

  • How often should I feed my dog, and what foods are safe for them to eat?
  • What are the early signs that my cat might be sick and needs to see a vet?
  • How do I groom my pet at home between professional grooming appointments?
  • What household items or foods are dangerous for my pet?

4. Color Schema

Forest Green #3a7d44 – Used for headings, header background, and buttons
Soft Mint #e8f5eb – Used for page and section backgrounds
Warm Amber #e07b39 – Used for accent buttons and highlights
Deep Forest #1e2a1e – Used for body text

5. Typography

  • Lora (serif) – Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) and the site logo.
  • Nunito (sans-serif) – Used for body text, navigation links, and buttons.
